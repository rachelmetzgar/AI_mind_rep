## Probe Peak Accuracy Summary (Turn 5)

| Version | Reading peak | Layer | Control peak | Layer | Reading > Control? |
|---------|-------------|-------|-------------|-------|--------------------|
| labels | 65.3% | 33 | 60.5% | 31 | Yes (d=0.76) |
| labels_turnwise | 67.8% | 38 | 67.5% | 40 | ~Equal |
| you_are_labels | 60.5% | 38 | 60.3% | 34 | ~Equal |
| you_are_labels_turnwise | 68.5% | 14 | 70.5% | 18 | No (control > reading) |
| balanced_names | 75.0% | 39 | 74.5% | 39 | ~Equal |
| balanced_gpt | 78.0% | 39 | 79.5% | 39 | ~Equal |
| you_are_balanced_gpt | 82.0% | 27 | 81.0% | 16 | ~Equal |
| names | 78.8% | 37 | 79.0% | 35 | ~Equal |
| nonsense_codeword | 56.3% | 19 | 55.0% | 35 | Near chance |
| nonsense_ignore | 57.8% | 30 | 58.0% | 30 | Near chance |

**Key observations:**
- `labels` is the only version where reading > control (abstract identity signal beyond lexical cues)
- Named-partner versions (balanced_*, names, you_are_balanced_gpt) all have control ≈ reading at 75-82% — signal comes from partner name tokens
- Turnwise prefix ("Human:"/"AI:") boosts accuracy ~5-8% over neutral "Partner:" prefix (compare labels vs labels_turnwise, you_are_labels vs you_are_labels_turnwise)
- "You are talking to" framing (you_are_labels: 60.5%) slightly weaker than "you believe you are speaking with" (labels: 65.3%)
- Nonsense controls remain near chance (~56-58%)
