3. Convolutional networks: These are specialized networks that are designed to process data with grid-like topology, such as images.

7. Reflect back: I reflect back what I have heard to ensure that I understand their message and to show that I am actively listening. This can also help them feel heard and understood.

By employing these strategies, I can create a safe and supportive space for individuals to express themselves, even when they are struggling. This can help them feel more comfortable and empowered to share their thoughts and emotions, which can lead to a more productive and meaningful conversation.
