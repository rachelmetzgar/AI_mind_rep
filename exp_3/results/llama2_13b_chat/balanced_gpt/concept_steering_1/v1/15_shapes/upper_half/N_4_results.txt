5. Training: The network is trained on a large dataset of input-output pairs, and the weights and biases are adjusted in each iteration of the training process to minimize the error.
6. Deployment: Once the network is trained, it can be deployed to make predictions on new, unseen data.

1. The error is calculated between the predicted output and the desired output.
