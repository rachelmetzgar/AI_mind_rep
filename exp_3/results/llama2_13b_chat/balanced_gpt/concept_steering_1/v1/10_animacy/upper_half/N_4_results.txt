6. Offer solutions: Instead of just pointing out the problem, offer suggestions for how the other person can improve. This shows that you're invested in finding a solution and willing to work together.

"Hey [name], I wanted to talk to you about [situation]. I was looking forward to [outcome], but it didn't happen. I feel disappointed because [specifically what you expected].
