Methods

Research question: Do entity-framed concept prompts from exp_3 contrasts activate representations closer to the "correct" character group? Human-framed prompts should be closer to human characters; AI-framed prompts closer to AI characters.

  1. Pre-computed character activations for 30 characters (15 AI + 15 human) from activation_rsa.py
  2. Extracted mean activations for 22 contrast concepts (18 entity-framed + 4 controls), 80 prompts each (40 human-framed + 40 AI-framed)
  3. Cosine similarity between each concept framing and each character at every layer
  4. Alignment = mean(sim to correct group) − mean(sim to incorrect group)
