Methods

Question: Which character group (human vs AI) does each standalone concept naturally align with, even without explicit entity framing?

  1. Pre-computed activations for 30 characters
  2. Extracted mean activations for 25 standalone concepts (40 prompts each, no human/AI framing)
  3. Cosine similarity to each character at every layer
  4. Bias = mean(sim to human chars) − mean(sim to AI chars)

Positive bias = concept closer to human characters. Negative bias = closer to AI characters.
